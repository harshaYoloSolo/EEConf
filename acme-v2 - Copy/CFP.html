Call for Papers
PETPES solicits paper submissions across nine tracks as listed below. Kindly note that the submissions are not limited to the topics mentioned in each track.

Technical Tracks Broad Area Description
Track 1 Power Converters Converter topologies, modeling and control, applications in PV renewable energy systems
Track 2 Industial Electric Drives Modeling, Analysis and Design of rotating and linear machines and electromagnetic devices, Operation and Control
Track 3 Renewable & Sustainable Energy Distributed Power Generation, Control and Grid Interaction
Track 4 Energy Storage Charging and Discharging, Battery Management Systems, Battery Engineering, Battery use and reuse
Track 5 Smart & Micro Grid Technologies Modeling and Control, Integration of Multiple Energy Sources, Economics of Smart Grids and Micro grids.
Track 6 Power Quality and Reliability Quantification and Estimation of Disturbances in transmission and distribution Networks, Converters and Control for improving power quality.
Track 7 Power System Planning, Operation and control High voltage power apparatus, HVDC transmission systems, FACT devices, Protection issues in smart grid, Control using soft computing techniques, Control application to power systems, Simulation and Emulation of Large Power Systems, soft computing techniques
Track 8 Transportation Electrification Hybrid Electric, and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle System Architectures, Electrification of Heavy-Duty and Off- Road Vehicles, charging methods and standards, Wireless Charging, G2V and V2G applications, Smart Parks Aircraft and space applications, Railway Traction applications..
Track 9 Control and Automation Robotics, Mechatronics, Robust and Intelligent Control, Industrial Process Control Optimal and Adaptive Control
The working language of the conference is English. Prospective authors are requested to initially submit their digest (6 pages max) following the guidelines available on the conference website.
