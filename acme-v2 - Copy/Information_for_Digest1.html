Paper Submission Details

Paper Submission Guidelines:
  • It is mandatory to submit the paper through EasyChair: Submission Link
  • Kindly note that the papers submitted via email will not be considered for review.
  • Since the "Accepted and presented papers" of PETPES-2019 will be eligible to be included in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, the manuscript must strictly adhere to the standard IEEE two column format and must not be more than 6 pages.
  • Typesetting instructions and sample templates: IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings
  • All submitted papers will be subject to thorough plagiarism check and the ones found with plagiarised content will not be reviewed further.
